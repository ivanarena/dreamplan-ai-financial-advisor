What is the reference date in the event of bankruptcy, reconstruction, cessation of operations and insolvency?
Read more and self-services
Bankruptcy or restructuring
In the event of bankruptcy or restructuring, the reference date is the day on which the probate court receives a request for initiating bankruptcy or restructuring.
See the date on the Danish Official Gazette:
Danish Official Gazette (in Danish)
Estate of a deceased person
For estates of deceased persons, the reference date is the date on which the employee died.
See the date on the Danish Official Gazette:
Danish Official Gazette (new window - in Danish)
Cessation of operations and insolvency
In the event of cessation of operations and insolvency, the reference date is the date specified by LG. Contact LG.
