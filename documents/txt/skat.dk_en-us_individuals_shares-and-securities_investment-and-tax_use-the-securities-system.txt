Use the securities system
By means of
the securities system (link in Danish)
, the Tax Agency automatically calculates the result of your investments in shares and investment certificates registered for trade in a regulated market, if the agency has the required information.
This is how you open the Securities system
Log on to E-tax (TastSelv)
Select
'Ret Årsopgørelsen/oplysningsskema' (Correct your tax assessment notice/tax return)
Click the calculator next to box 30, 38, 66 or 345
You will then enter the securities system and you will see your investments if we have the required information. You can see an example here:
