See which information we already have about your investments
Once you have logged on to E-tax (TastSelv), you can select
'Skatteoplysninger' (Tax information)
. In the menu, you can select your type of investment and see which information is automatically reported by your bank or the platform you use.
In
'Skatteoplysninger'
you can also see if your investment is registered for trade.
Read more about when shares are registered for trade (currently in Danish)
.
It is your responsibility to make sure that the information is correct.
Please note that even if there is information about your investments in
'Skatteoplysninger',
it is not cure that we can base a calculation of profit or loss on it.
This is why it is important that you always check your tax assessment notice and report your profit or loss, if it is not already stated.
