Register outstanding tax and B-tax for Betalingsservice
You can register your giro transfer forms to be paid automatically via Betalingsservice (nets direct debit service) in two ways: Via a specific link to
betalingsservice.dk
or via your bank or online banking. You can follow our step-by-step guide below.
Once the payments appear from your Betalingsservice statement, they will be paid automatically.
Register your B-tax and labour market contributions at betalingsservice.dk
B-tax can be ordinary B-tax, included outstanding tax, penalty interest and property value tax.
Go to ’Tilmeld’ (Register) at betalingsservice.dk (’B-skat og am-bidrag’ (B-tax and labour market contributions))
Complete the fields using your CPR no. and your banking information (registration no. and account no.)
Enter your CPR no. again in the field "16xxxxxx9xxxx25" where the xs are your CPR no.
Confirm your registration and your information by signing with your MitID. You can only register payments from your own account.
Register your personal taxes (B-tax, outstanding tax, labour market contributions and outstanding labour market contributions) at betalingsservice.dk
Register (all personal taxes) at betalingsservice.dk
Complete the fields using your CPR no. and your banking information (registration no. and account no.)
Enger your CPR no. again in the field "16xxxxxx9xxxx25" where the xs are your CPR no.
Confirm your registration and your information by signing with your MitID. You can only register payments from your own account.
You can also register with Betalingsservice by giving this information to your bank or online banking.

Deadline for registering or cancelling your payment by Betalingsservice is the 6
th
last banking day of the month at 9 a.m. For further information in Danish on deadlines please see
betalingsservice.dk
.
Please note that you may be unable to pay outstanding tax from earlier years via Betalingsservice. In this case, please make your payment via E-tax (TastSelv).
