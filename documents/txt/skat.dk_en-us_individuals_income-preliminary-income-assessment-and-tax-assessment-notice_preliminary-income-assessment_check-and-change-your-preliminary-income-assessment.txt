Changing your assessment means that you manage your tax affairs:
You avoid a tax bill in your tax assessment notice for 2026
You pay the correct tax each month
You get the deductions you are entitled to
Your tax card will be adjusted accordingly
See and change your preliminary income assessment
Did you buy or sell a home?
Remember to change your preliminary income assessment (forskudsopgørelse) if you buy or sell a home. Property owners are charged property tax monthly, so if you change your assessment you will be paying the correct tax every month.
In 2025, you pay housing tax on the provisional 2024 assessment. This assessment is an adjusted version of your public property assessment for 2022. It has been adjusted according to the prices in property market of 2022-2024.
How to see your housing tax in your preliminary income assessment
Are you entitled to a transport deduction?
Remember to enter your deduction for transport from home to work in your preliminary income assessment in field 417.
You are entitled to a deduction if you travel more than 24 km daily to and from work that you are being paid to do. Maybe your commute changed in distance? Check if your way to an from work entitle you to a deduction, and enter your days of travel in your assessment.
